# Care-Caddy
CareCaddy is an online medicine shopping platform designed especially for patients who take medicines for lifelong conditions. The website allows users to browse and order medicines easily, track expiry dates, receive refill reminders, and connect with healthcare agencies for monitoring support. Care Caddy focuses on timely home delivery, medicine safety, and continuous care management in a simple and user-friendly way.
The platform ensures that patients never miss a dose by sending smart notifications and maintaining a digital medication history. It also provides secure prescription storage, making reordering quick and hassle-free. CareCaddy enhances transparency by allowing users to check medicine availability, compare prices, and verify authenticity.
By integrating technology with healthcare services, CareCaddy reduces the stress of managing chronic conditions and improves overall patient compliance. The system is designed to support elderly users and caregivers with an intuitive interface and accessible features.
Overall, CareCaddy aims to create a reliable, safe, and convenient healthcare ecosystem that promotes better health outcomes and peace of mind for patients and their families.
